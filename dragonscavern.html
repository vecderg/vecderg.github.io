FIDGET'S UNIVERSE


A universe full of magic, dragons, and the potential for adventure!

Fidget Habanogard is a young red panda-dragon who's born to a legendary hero. He wants to follow in his footsteps, but realizes that it may be more difficult than expected...

Fidget's Universe, currently unnamed, features many characters, and even a few short stories! The universe was originally planned to have a full comic, but it would just take way too long. The story only exists in pieces of written form for now, which honestly might suit it better.

CHARACTERS

Until pages are made for EVERY SINGLE CHARACTER, please refer to this page: https://toyhou.se/vecderg/characters/folder:1510843


Fidget Habanogard - The protagonist, a red panda-dragon with fire magic eager to prove himself a hero.

Kraktid Habanogard - Fidget's father, a legendary hero with lightning powers.

Soren Daranogard - Fidget's childhood friend and rival, a prankster with portal powers.

Bellumon Daranogard - Kraktid's lifelong friend, a large and friendly dragon with deadly combat abilities.


Taro - A tribal fennec-kobold with outstanding artistic ability, mainly in sculpting. Friends with Kraktid.

Ube - Taro's only son. Has no powers and wields a spear for self-defense. Likes masks and collecting rocks!

Hyde - A cow-skunk wanderer who enjoys new sights and meeting new people. Has air-based powers.

Pond - A goat-rabbit with force field powers. Still in mourning for his friends.

Scrampy - A Scrampjaw tamed by Fidget. Dog-like personality with the body of a hulking fish monster.
