CONLANGS


Conlangs are constructed languages, languages that were created and designed intentionally rather than naturally. This has become a big interest of mine! There's something so cool to me about how languages can be designed -- especially with auxlangs (languages designed to be an international auxiliary language), there are a lot of pros and cons to different design elements.

If you want a thorough summary on my thoughts on every auxlang I've studied, read this: https://www.vecderg.com/articles/auxlangs

And my thoughts on auxlang design in general: https://www.vecderg.com/articles/auxlangs2

I also made a conlang of my own! It's called Kaliwe, and it's what my character Ube speaks (he's the lil guy that should be attached to your cursor right now, if you're on desktop): https://www.vecderg.com/kaliwe


If you want any of my other conlang content (e.g. resources I created for Mini-Linga), scroll down to the conlang section of my article page: https://www.vecderg.com/articles
