Dasopya Dictionary



> About

Dasopya is a constructed language created for international communication. Visit the official website for more information!

- Whole Word Search: Reads the search as a complete word (e.g. "a" will return "a reading" but not "reading")
- Include Compound Words: Includes compounds in search results (words containing 2 or more root words)
- Include Loanwords: Includes words that aren't part of the base vocabulary (country names, loanwords, letter names, etc.)

Note that in Dasopya, compound words are not lexicalized (e.g. rail-vehicle just means rail-vehicle, but is listed as "train" because that's considered one of the most likely interpretations). The compound words listed here are just recommendations.
