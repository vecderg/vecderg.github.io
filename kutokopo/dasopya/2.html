Sample Text

oEma: bamu!
oDani: bamu.
oEma: bay e oEma. kyu e?
oDani: bay e oDani.
oEma: gadu telu pya. bamu!
oDani: bamu!

Vocabulary:

  • bamu = hello, goodbye
  • bay = I, me, my
  • kyu = you, your
  • gadu = thank you
  • telu = to, for
  • pya = talk, speech, language

  • bay e... = I am...
  • kyu e? = You are?
  • gadu telu pya = Thanks for the talk.


Words in Dasopya

Every base word in Dasopya is only 1-2 syllables. Words that aren't included in the base vocabulary (loanwords, like names) are marked with the prefix o-, and these words can be longer than 2 syllables.

Capitalization is fully optional in Dasopya! Normally, all words are lowercase unless it's marked with the o- prefix, since these are usually proper nouns.

This sample text uses particles that will be explained in a later lesson in more detail.
