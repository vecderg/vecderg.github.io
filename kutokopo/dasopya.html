Preface

Dasopya is a constructed language made by Vecderg in 2024 with the purpose of creating a simple and truly neutral international language. It is a priori and oligosynthetic, meaning that every word is made from scratch, and every word can be combined freely with each other to make complex compound words. There are about 800 base words in total.

There are many tutorials for the language, including a 5 minute overview, a 1-page cheat sheet, and a Beginner's Lessons document. However, as the first 2 resources are meant to be a quick introduction to the language, and the latter is more of a thorough focus on grammar, this tutorial will try to introduce both vocabulary and grammar in a more natural manner.

These lesson formats are based strongly on the Esperanto and Lingua Franca Nova Wikibooks tutorials.
