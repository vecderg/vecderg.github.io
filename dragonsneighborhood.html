PLUCK'S UNIVERSE


A world full of anthros and a touch of idealism, which is otherwise nearly identical to our own. At the center of this wonderful world is a lizard who won't let himself be happy.

Pluck's Universe, currently unnamed, features a number of characters. The universe was originally planned to have a full comic, but I'm unsure if it will ever be made.


CHARACTERS

Until pages are made for EVERY SINGLE CHARACTER, please refer to this page: https://toyhou.se/vecderg/characters/folder:1510839


Pluck Luro - A wingless dragon with talent and potential that he's too "lazy" to fulfill.

Doodle Felder - Young loud-mouthed cat that Pluck had the unfortunate chance to babysit.

Fumble Chroto - Young bat who's Doodle's best friend, shy and self-conscious about his wing-arms.

Baxter Collie - Classmate of Pluck that's friendly and outgoing, happening to be in the same club as him.

Unnamed Possum / Skunk - Friends of Doodle.


Daymond Platinum - Rebel fox who loves retro tech (tentatively placed in Pluck's Universe)
