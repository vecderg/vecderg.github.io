- Konakin - PICO-8 Game

The first Dasopya game, open-world Frogger!

After learning about Frogger's history, I wanted to make a game based on it. I unburied the idea after brainstorming what would be a good fit for the first Dasopya game -- one that could carry a notable amount of text without requiring fluency. All the text is in my auxlang Dasopya, but you can still play it with 0 knowledge!
