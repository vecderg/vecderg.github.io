Hi, I'm Vecderg!

I'm a gamedev/artist/Youtuber person who just kind of likes doing whatever catches my interest.

I was inspired bythis videoto make a website of my own. If you know me, you may know I have a LOT of websites, but none that I truly have full control over. You can check out my websitevecderg.comto get a much more polished-looking website with all of my social links, but this is the place where I will do literally whatever I want.

This is where I will practice HTML, this is where I will ramble about my OCs, this is where I will allow myself and my website to look kinda cringe.

And that's because it's fun! I didn't quite get to experience the Web 1.0 era, and I have a particular liking for retro aesthetics. I hope to be able to make my sort of contribution to the new old web with my own little spin on things.
